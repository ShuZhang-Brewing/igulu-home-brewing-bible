# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The iGulu Home Brewing Bible is a community-driven project. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for all contributors, regardless of background, experience level, nationality, or brewing preference.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Write original content — no plagiarism, copying, or paraphrasing from other sources without proper attribution
- Be respectful and constructive when reviewing or revising others' contributions
- Provide accurate information to the best of your knowledge
- Clearly disclose when iGulu device parameters are estimated vs. tested
- Respect the editorial team's decisions on content quality and acceptance

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Submitting plagiarized or AI-generated content as original work
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Deliberately submitting inaccurate or harmful brewing information
- Attempting to circumvent the CLA or intellectual property terms
- Spamming or submitting low-quality content to farm rewards

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. A warning from the editorial team
2. Removal of the offending content
3. Temporary suspension of contribution privileges
4. Permanent ban from the project

iGulu reserves the right to determine appropriate consequences at its sole discretion.

---

## Reporting

To report a violation, open an Issue with the label `conduct` or contact the editorial team directly.

---

*This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within the iGulu Home Brewing Bible project, including GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, and any related community spaces.*

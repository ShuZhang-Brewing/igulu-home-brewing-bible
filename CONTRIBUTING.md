# Contributing to iGulu Home Brewing Bible

Thank you for your interest in contributing! This guide explains everything you need to know to submit a recipe chapter.

---

## Before You Start

1. **Sign the CLA** — All contributors must sign the Contributor License Agreement before their first Pull Request will be reviewed. The CLA Assistant bot will prompt you automatically.
2. **Request access** — This is a private repository. Submit an access request via Issues.
3. **Read the template** — Every submission must follow the standard format in TEMPLATE.md.

---

## Submission Rules

- Maximum 5 original chapters per contributor
- Chapters must be written in English
- Minimum 1,500 words, maximum 3,000 words per chapter
- Content must be original
- iGulu device parameters must be based on actual testing

---

## How to Submit

1. Fork this repository
2. Create a new branch: git checkout -b recipe/your-recipe-name
3. Write your chapter using TEMPLATE.md
4. Submit a Pull Request titled: [New Recipe] Your Recipe Name
5. Sign the CLA when prompted
6. Wait for editorial review within 7 business days

---

## Review Process

Stage | Timeline | Action
Initial check | 1-2 days | Format and CLA verification
Editorial review | 3-5 days | Content quality and accuracy
iGulu parameter check | 1-2 days | Device compatibility review
Merge and reward | Same day | Points credited to your account

---

## Rewards

Original chapter approved: 500 pts
Quality revision: 50-200 pts
Complete 5 chapters: Bonus 1,000 pts

Points are credited to your iGulu account within 24 hours of merge.

---

## Questions?

Open an issue or contact the editorial team.
